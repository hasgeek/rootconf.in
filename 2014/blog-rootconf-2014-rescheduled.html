Rootconf 2014 is rescheduled

by Kiran Jonnalagadda · Mar 6, 2014, 8:47:07 PM

The Election Commission has announced the dates for the next Lok Sabha elections and Karnataka is going to the polls on April 17, bang in the middle of Rootconf. Other states are +/- a week or two.

We feel this will be rather disruptive for the event, and we do believe all of us should be going to vote, so we're rescheduling Rootconf to a month later. The conference will now be on May 16 and 17, with workshops on May 14 and 15.

Looking at the brighter side, having a full extra month also means we have that much more time to make this an even awesomer event, so I hope you will become more involved, helping select talks and getting your friends to participate.
